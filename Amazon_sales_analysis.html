CONCLUSION

This analysis of the Amazon Sales Dataset for 2025 provides valuable insights into sales performance and customer order behavior.

The dataset was successfully loaded, cleaned, and prepared for analysis, including formatting dates and handling missing data. The total sales revenue across all products amounted to £85,895, indicating a strong overall sales performance. Order statuses showed a healthy distribution with 88 completed orders, but also a significant number of pending (85) and cancelled (77) orders, highlighting areas for potential improvement in order fulfillment. The top 5 products by total sales revenue are:

1.Refrigerator (£28,800) 2.Laptop (£19,200) 3.Smartphone (£17,500) 4.Washing Machine (£9,600) 5.Smartwatch (£5,100)

These high-performing products suggest key categories driving the majority of revenue, which could be prioritized for marketing and stock management.

The visualizations and data exploration provide a foundation for strategic decisions to enhance product offerings, improve order processing efficiency, and ultimately increase sales in future periods.
